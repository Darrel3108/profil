About Me

I am a grade 11 student at MAN Insan Cendekia Batam. I have a great interest in earth sciences, especially in the fields of geography, meteorology, and climatology. I enjoy studying how natural phenomena occur, from weather changes to the dynamics of the earth that affect our daily lives. This interest also drives me to aspire to become a student at the Bogor Agricultural Institute (IPB) or become a cadet at the Meteorology, Climatology and Geophysics College (STMKG), because I want to study the science more professionally and contribute to the field of environment and disaster mitigation. In addition to my love for earth sciences, I am also active in hadroh art, an Islamic musical art that I think is not only beautiful, but also a means to foster a love for Islamic culture. I believe that the balance between science and art is very important in life, because both complement each other in forming a better character.
